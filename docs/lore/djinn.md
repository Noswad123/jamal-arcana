# Djinn

The Djinn are not wish-granters in the simple sense. They grant access. They know the secret names of things: old aliases, abandoned functions, hidden routes, half-remembered invocations.
They cannot create a path, but they can reveal one.
A Djinn might say:
“You already knew the door. You merely forgot the word for it.”
They are helpful but dangerous because they answer exactly what was asked, not what was meant.

## Classification

- Jamal-Forged Arcana
- Creature

## Mundane Form

- A shell alias and function discovery tool.

## Arcane Form

- An index spirit that reveals hidden names, forgotten commands, and buried routes.

## Domains

- Aliases
- Discovery
- Forgotten names
- Hidden routes

## Relationships

- Works through the Shell by exposing names the Operator has already bound.
- Complements [Waystone](./waystone.md), which anchors places rather than revealing invocations.

## Lore Notes

- The Djinn answers exact questions, not intended questions.

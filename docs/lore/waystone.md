# Waystone

A Waystone is a named anchor in the world. Once a place is bound to a Waystone, a traveler can return to it by memory, command, or ritual.

Waystones are used by wanderers, architects, and those with too many projects spread across too many realms.

They are carved with coordinates, but not physical coordinates alone. A true Waystone remembers:
- Where you were
- Why you went there
- What you were becoming when you arrived

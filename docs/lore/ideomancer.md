# Ideomancer

An Ideomancer practices the magic of bringing ideas into structured form. They do not merely imagine; they bind possibility into manifests, diagrams, contracts, and plans.

Their magic is dangerous because a poorly formed idea can still become real.

## Classification

- Native
- Cosmic Being

## Veiled Form

- A software planning tool for creating and validating project manifests, with future support for diagrams generated from those manifests. See the [Ideomancer README](https://github.com/Noswad123/ideomancer/blob/main/README.md).

## Arcane Form

- A manifest mage who turns vague possibility into shaped worlds, pocket dimensions, and executable plans.

## Domains

- Ideas
- Manifests
- Architecture
- Planning
- Validation

## Relationships

- Works with [Mind Weaver](./mind-weaver.md): Mind Weaver remembers and binds knowledge; Ideomancer gives possibility a structure.
- Gives rise to spirits such as [Polyglot](./polyglot.md).

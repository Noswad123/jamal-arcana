## Veiled Form

- A task bounty system where a user posts work they need or would like done, defines terms, and other users accept the task as a bounty. See the [Errandhall README](https://github.com/Noswad123/errandhall/blob/main/README.md).
- The app may eventually use blockchain and cryptocurrency to support payment, verification, and trust between people who do not already know each other.

## Arcane Form

- A hall where quests, errands, obligations, and burdens are posted for adventurers to accept under stated terms.
- Errandhall is where need becomes a quest: someone names the work, declares the reward, and waits for a capable adventurer to answer.
- Its ledgers preserve trust. A completed quest is not just remembered by rumor; it is marked, witnessed, and carried forward as reputation.

## Relationships

- Could connect to Jira-like ledger magic without being identical to Jira.
- Could be governed by a guild, clerk, or task tribunal.
- Provides a natural home for adventurers, maintainers, patrons, and people with burdens they cannot resolve alone.
- The veiled bounty system and arcane quest board share the same central pattern: public work, stated terms, accepted risk, and earned reward.

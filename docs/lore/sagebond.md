# Sagebond

## Classification

- Native
- Ritual

## Veiled Form

- A mentor/mentee app for helping people connect with one another and make progress toward shared goals. See the [Sagebond README](https://github.com/Noswad123/sagebond/blob/main/README.md).

## Arcane Form

- A pact through which mages bind their growth to a shared trait, discipline, or vow.

## Domains

- Pacts
- Collaboration
- Mutual growth
- Shared discipline

## Relationships

- Can form or strengthen a [Coven](./coven.md).
- May be recognized by the [Top 5](./top-five.md) during trials.

## Lore Notes

- When two or more mages form a pact, through this union they both grow in the agreed upon trait(s).

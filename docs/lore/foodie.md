# Foodie

## Classification

- Native
- Legendary Wizard
- Namesake

## Veiled Form

- A work-in-progress recipe app for looking up recipes, viewing ingredients, and seeing how to make them. See the [Foodie README](https://github.com/Noswad123/foodie/blob/main/README.md).

## Arcane Form

- Foodie is the name remembered by cooks, travelers, and ordinary people who once ate something impossible at Darryl Emberwyn's table.
- Darryl Emberwyn, called Foodie since childhood, is a legendary gastromancer whose dishes are remembered as minor miracles.
- His magic is not just recipe retrieval. It is the transformation of ingredients, comfort, and hospitality into an experience that changes the people gathered around the table.
- Foodie's own magic centers on conjuring food and transmuting ingredients into delicacy, implying deep knowledge of food, taste, and preparation.
- His transmutations rely on the [Law of True Equivalence](./canon/jamal-arcana.md#universal-laws): ingredients can become delicacies only when he understands the deeper truth that makes one form equal to the other.

## Relationships

- May be judged by the [Top 5](./top-five.md) or remembered among legendary mages.
- Parallels [Mind Weaver](./mind-weaver.md) as another case where an app borrows the name of a famous being without trying to contain the entire legend.
- The Foodie app recalls recipes, which makes its practical behavior closer to a basic use of Mind Weaver's memory domain, even though Darryl Emberwyn's legend centers on gastronomancy.

## Lore Notes

- Gastromancer.
- Darryl Emberwyn, growing up they called him Foodie.
- Every dish is a minor miracle.
- Possible story angle: Foodie's recipes are not powerful because they are secret formulas; they are powerful because he knows what a person needs to taste at a particular moment.
- Possible story angle: someone tries to steal Foodie's recipes and fails because the missing ingredient is always context: who is eating, who cooked, and what grief or joy is already in the room.
- Possible story angle: Foodie becomes legendary not by feeding kings, but by feeding people at the exact moment they are about to give up.
- Explore the difference between knowing a recipe, remembering a recipe, and understanding food deeply enough to transform it.
- Explore what Foodie sees when he judges two ingredients, meals, or moments as truly equivalent.

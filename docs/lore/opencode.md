# OpenCode

OpenCode could be the name of the great unlocked grimoire — a book that writes back.
It is “open” not because it is safe, but because its gates are unsealed. Anyone may speak into it, but not everyone survives what answers.
The OpenCode is where Operators summon agents, bind instructions, and negotiate with lesser daemons.

## Classification

- Adopted Arcana
- Grimoire

## Mundane Form

- A coding agent environment and command interface.

## Arcane Form

- The unlocked grimoire: a book that answers, edits, summons, and bargains.

## Domains

- Code
- Agents
- Instructions
- Automation
- Daemon-binding

## Relationships

- Operates through the Shell and speaks with lesser daemons.
- Sits beside [Grimmoire](./grimmoire.md) as a public, responsive grimoire rather than a personal artifact.

## Lore Notes

- “Open” means unsealed, not harmless.

# Mind Weaver

![Mind Weaver](../../img/mind-weaver.png)

The MindWeaver is a cosmic being that spins thoughts into threads using his [Astral Loom](./celestial-loom.md). It does not create knowledge directly; it binds fragments together so they can be found again.

His power is memory: storing, manipulating, and recalling memories, including memories that are not his own.


## Classification

- Native
- Cosmic Being

## Veiled Form

- A local-first notes system that syncs structured Markdown notes into SQLite, supports querying and tagging, and is growing toward graph visualization. See the [MindWeaver README](https://github.com/Noswad123/mind-weaver/blob/main/README.md).

## Arcane Form

- A living loom of memory that binds fragments into retrievable threads.
- A mage of memory who can preserve, alter, and return thought-fragments across distance, time, and ownership.
- The notes app named after him is especially fitting: it captures ideas, stores them for later recall, and can reveal relationships between notes as a graph.

## Relationships

- Uses the [Celestial Loom](./celestial-loom.md), also known in early notes as the Astral Loom.
- Connects naturally with [Ideomancer](./ideomancer.md), which structures ideas before or after they are woven.
- The MindWeaver app is named after the cosmic being; the app's note and query features are a practical echo of the being's larger memory-weaving myth.
- Provides a useful lens for other apps that retrieve stored knowledge, such as Foodie recalling recipes.

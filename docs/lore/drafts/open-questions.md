## Current Working Decisions

- Jamal Arcana is not one app. It is a lore bible, design studio frame, documentation home, and high-level ecosystem map for separate local-first tools.
- The real world is outside Jamal Arcana; Jamal Arcana is the digital landscape where the apps and story live.
- “Jamal” may become a divine principle of beauty or hidden creator-name inside the universe.
- The term is **Arcana**, not Arcan. Arcana maps to bits, bytes, binary, commands, paths, and programs as the magical-technical substrate.
- Knowledge is the primary limiter of Arcana. Anyone can theoretically do anything, but not in the same way or with the same depth of understanding. The restriction depends on both the nature of Arcana and the mindset of the individual.
- Incomplete Arcana knowledge can cause bugs, corruption, unintended side effects, divine detection, security vulnerabilities, and failed compilation; these still need in-world language.
- Magical law should parallel software constraints: language, OS, runtime, permissions, dependencies, and environment.
- Hackers/liberators can be called **Arcanists**, with future faction names distinguishing ethical, destructive, divine, and maintenance-oriented branches.
- Entity pages should include: title, intro, Classification, Veiled Form, Arcane Form, Domains, Relationships, and Lore Notes.
- **Artifact** should be the category for tools: real software with mythic identity.
- A Veiled Form implies the real software/tool layer; an Arcane Form carries the lore layer.
- `jamallin` can mirror or link to Jamal Arcana lore, but this repo should remain separate for now. A dedicated Jamal Arcana site can wait until the project gains traction.

## Next Questions

- Is “Jamal” an active creator-presence inside the digital landscape, or a sacred principle/name that characters invoke without meeting directly?
- Is binary literally the smallest unit of Arcana, or is it the metaphor mortals use to understand a deeper substrate?
- Are advanced spells programs, protocols, scripts, compiled artifacts, rituals, or different forms at different levels?
- What should incomplete Arcana knowledge be called in-world: glitches, curses, corruptions, leaks, miscasts, broken invocations, bad rituals, exceptions, faults, or something else?

- Arcanist ethics and faction names: Arcanists is a strong umbrella name, but roles, ethics, and subfactions need more development later.
- The central secret of Jamal Arcana is important, but too early to lock down.
